About Us

We are both Chartered Counselling Psychologists and Psychoanalytic Psychotherapists. We have PhDs in Psychology and are also Chartered Scientists.

We were trained at the British Association of Psychotherapists and are registered with the British Psychological Society, the United Kingdom Council for Psychotherapy, the Health and Care Professions Council, the Forum for Independent Psychotherapists and the Council for Psychoanalysis and Jungian Analysis.

We are also registered with Private Insurers AXA, PPP and others.

Dr. Aslan Mordecai

PhD, CPsychol, CClin Psychol, C Sci, AFBPsS

I have worked in:

  • A Prison
  • An Approved School (similar to a Borstal)
  • Child Guidance
  • Clinic for Group, Marital and Sex Therapy
  • Mental Hospitals
  • Psychiatric Clinics

Dr. Kay Mordecai

PhD, CPsychol, C Sci, AFBPsS

My Background:

I initially trained as a Psychologist and did a PhD on “Maternal Depression during Pregnancy and after Childbirth”. It was my experience of talking in depth to troubled mothers that made me decide to train as a psychotherapist at the BAP. I knew from my research that Just Talking To Someone really does help alleviate depression.
