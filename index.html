Welcome to London Psychotherapists

Chartered Counselling Psychologists, Psychoanalytic Psychotherapists and Chartered Scientists, offering Psychotherapy and Counselling in North London.

We work individually, seeing adults for short term counselling and longer term psychotherapy in a calm, safe and friendly environment. We are dedicated to providing a warm, effective and professional service in comfortable consulting rooms.

We are North London Psychotherapists, trying to help you through life’s problems without judgement. Everything discussed is kept strictly confidential.

First Therapy Session Free – Immediate Bookings Available

This meeting allows us the opportunity to discuss the counselling process and the reasons you are seeking it. It also allows you to assess the environment and learn what to expect from continued counselling. To find out more about what our sessions offer, you can view the Therapy Session page.

Contact us today to book a free therapy session. For a full list of our services please see What We Do.
